Objective
🎯 What you will prove in this exam

Master the hash map as a tool for trading space for time. Every problem here has a brute-force O(n²) solution and an elegant O(n) solution. Your job is to find the O(n) path — and know why a map makes it possible.

💡
The core insight of this entire exam: A map answers "have I seen this before?" in O(1). That single idea — storing what you've seen so you don't have to search again — converts dozens of O(n²) problems into O(n). Every question here is a variation of that idea.
